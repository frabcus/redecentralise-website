Re­decentra­lize Conference 2019

25 October 2019 — London

Interested in a fairer, freer web and net? Working on decentralisation? Care about privacy, automomy and our future digital infrastructure?

Then join us to learn, compare experiences, bounce ideas, share challenges and lessons learnt, hatch collaborations, plan campaigns and start projects.

What

We had an amazing time at our first, sold-out conference in 2015 and are finally hosting a similar event. It will be a one-day unconference in London on 25th October.

We’ll have open sessions, lightning talks and workshops. In unconference style, you can pitch your session on the day itself; or feel free to already get in touch with an idea. Some topics already in motion:

  • UX patterns: exchanging good practices for smooth user experiences in decentralised software
  • Decent lobbying: shall we campaign together in Brussels/Westminster/etc?
  • Mapping the space: creating an awesome-list to oversee themes and projects
  • Interoperability and open standards: from planning to action
  • The what, why, and how of decentralised identity
  • Surviving in a world of platforms: economic models that don’t depend on scale

When

Friday 25 October. This is the day before MozFest, so come for both!

We gather from 10am to 6pm (and go for drinks or MozFest pre-partying afterwards).
